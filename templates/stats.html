Price Drivers
PRODUCT PRICE SENSETIVITY

Product price sensitivity refers to the extent to which changes in the price of a product or service affect consumer demand for that product. It is a measure of how responsive consumers are to price fluctuations. Understanding product price sensitivity is crucial for businesses when determining pricing strategies and optimizing sales volume and revenue.

CONSUMER PRICE SENSETIVITY

Market price sensitivity pertains to the responsiveness of the overall market or industry to changes in product prices. It involves analyzing how price changes can influence market demand, competitive dynamics, and market share. Market price sensitivity is vital for businesses to assess market conditions, anticipate competitive responses, and make informed pricing decisions to stay competitive and maximize profitability.

MARKET PRICE SENSETIVITY

Consumer price sensitivity focuses on the reactions and behaviors of individual consumers towards price changes. It examines how sensitive consumers are to price variations and how their purchasing decisions are influenced by price levels. Understanding consumer price sensitivity helps businesses determine optimal pricing levels, identify price thresholds, and develop effective pricing strategies that align with consumer preferences and maximize customer satisfaction and loyalty.
